Gluttonous Baked Potatoes

About These Potatoes

There comes a time in everyones life, where canned goods provide comfort. After trying this recipe you will drool every time you see a Man-Wich can of gravy-whatever-it-is.

I learned this easy recipe on a tugboat way back when, and I still enjoy it.

Substitute stew meat if you have time (and simmer) and it is also very good with wild game.

Ingredients

  • Washed russet potatoes 3-5
  • Can Manwich sloppy joe
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lg onion chopped
  • 1tb butter
  • 1/4c flour
  • salt and pepper
  • sour cream for topping
  • green onion for topping
  • 1 can kidney beans

Steps

  1. Turn oven to 450 and put potatoes in
  2. Put large saucepan to med-high heat
  3. Add butter, coat bottom of pan
  4. Add onion, reduce down
  5. Add 1Tb of water to pan if onions stick
  6. Add ground beef and cook down
  7. Add flour and mix in to absorb grease
  8. Add kidney beans and manwich
  9. Simmer on low until potatoes are tender
  10. Cut potatoes into chunks
  11. Add a ladle of glory and top as you like
  12. Enjoy a well deserved nap!
