Sandwich (The Best)

About This Sandwich

I always love a good sandwich, but sometimes the regular mayo and lunchmeat just doesn't cut it.

Here is one of my favorites, that's just as easy (if not easier) and you get a hot meal at the end

If you want extra goblin points, I suggest serving with an assortment of ketchup, ranch, and hot mustard.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Fresh ham med slice
  • Good soft cheese or two (gruyere anyone?)
  • Mayo
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat pan up (med)
  2. Lather every surface of bread in mayo
  3. Add salt and pepper, and pop in pan
  4. When toasted, flip and add cheese
  5. Add meat to pan to warm
  6. Add warm meat and assemble
  7. Let sit off pan for 1min then halve
  8. Indulge!
