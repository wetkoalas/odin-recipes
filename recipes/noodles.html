About These Noodles

These noodles are a great meal any time of the day. I prefer them in the afternoon, but any time is a great time for noodles!

I usually use dried japanese noodles (soba), but you can use any type of dried or fresh noodle depending on your mood.

These taste great hot or cold, so make wonderful leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Handful of cooked or fresh noodles
  • 1tsp sesame oil
  • 1Tb Hoisin
  • 1tsp salt and pepper
  • 1Tb soy sauce
  • 1tsp dark soy sauce
  • handful of thai basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat pan and add sesame oil
  2. Add noodles, salt, and pepper
  3. Add dark soy, and soy, sautee until coated
  4. Add hoisin, stir, and garnish with thai basil
  5. Enjoy!
